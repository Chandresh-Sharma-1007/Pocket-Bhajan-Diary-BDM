About Pocket Bhajan Diary
📱 About the App – Pocket Diary Bhajans (BDM)

Pocket Diary Bhajans (BDM) is a devotional mobile application designed as a digital pocket diary of lyrics for Bhajans and Aartis. The app primarily features spiritual content dedicated to the Goddess Durga, Lord Hanuman, and the revered social saint Akharam Ji Maharaj (Also known as Dadaji).

This application is a heartfelt tribute to God and also an initiative to promote a paperless and eco-friendly approach, helping reduce the use of printed books and save trees.

The idea and vision of this app were conceptualised by Ashok Kumar Sharma & Ramesh Kumar Sharma (Sharma Brothers), who aimed to preserve devotional content in a modern digital format.

The application has been developed and coded by Chandresh Sharma and Himanshu Mishra, transforming this vision into a user-friendly digital experience.

With a curated collection of Bhajans and Aartis in one place, the app allows devotees to easily access and read lyrics anytime, anywhere, creating a simple, centralised, and peaceful devotional experience.

✨ From tradition to technology — preserving devotion in a modern digital form.
